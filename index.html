Notes On The Global Affairs





1948

The USA manipulates elections in Italy to hinder the communist party from winning.

The French Fourth Republic collapses and the French Fifth Republic emerges. France gives the colonies the choice of autonomy in a new French Community or immediate independence. Guinea votes for immediate independence. To stop other countries from choosing immediate independence, France tears down all that they consider their contributions to Guinea (unscrew lightbulbs, remove plans for sewage pipelines in Conakry, the capital, and even burn medicines rather than leave them for the Guineans).
