1948

The USA manipulates elections in Italy to hinder the communist party from winning.

France gives the colonies the choice of autonomy in a new French Community or immediate independence.

Guinea votes for immediate independence. To stop other countries from going the same path, France tears down all that they consider their contributions to Guinea (unscrew lightbulbs, remove plans for sewage pipelines in Conakry, the capital, and even burn medicines rather than leave them for the Guineans).
